The {\ttfamily compare} method must be implemented in derived classes. It is used to determine the priority of two elements, where {\ttfamily true} indicates that {\ttfamily t1} is considered to have a higher priority than {\ttfamily t2}, and {\ttfamily false} means {\ttfamily t2} has higher priority or they are equal.
